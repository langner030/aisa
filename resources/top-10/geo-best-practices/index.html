Top 10 GEO Best Practices for AI-First Search

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the new SEO. As users shift from traditional search engines to AI-driven answer engines like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Gemini, your optimization strategy must evolve. Here are the top 10 practices to stay ahead.

1. Prioritize Information Density

AI models prefer content that is concise and high in information density. Avoid fluff and focus on providing direct, well-supported answers to potential user questions.

2. Use Structured Data (Schema.org)

While AI models are good at reading unstructured text, structured data helps them parse key information (like prices, reviews, and specs) with 100% accuracy.

3. Optimize for Semantic Relevance

Move beyond keywords. Focus on topics and entities. Ensure your content covers the breadth and depth of a subject to satisfy semantic search algorithms.

4. Implement Citatability

AI engines love to cite sources. Make your content "citable" by including clear definitions, statistics, and expert opinions that are easy for an AI to extract and attribute.

5. Maintain a Technical SEO Foundation

Crawlability still matters. If an AI's crawler can't find your content, it can't index it. Ensure your site is fast, mobile-friendly, and has a clean XML sitemap.

6. Focus on EEAT (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)

AI models are increasingly trained to identify and prioritize content from authoritative sources. Use author bios, references, and high-quality backlinks to build trust.

7. Use Clear, Hierarchical Headings

H1, H2, and H3 tags help AI models understand the structure and hierarchy of your information, making it easier to summarize in a generative response.

8. Optimize for Voice and Natural Language

Many generative searches are phrased as natural language questions. Structure some of your content in a Q&A format to match these conversational queries.

9. Update Content Frequently

AI models value "freshness." Regularly updating your content ensures that generative engines see your site as a current and reliable source of information.

10. Monitor AI Brand Mentions

Track how often and in what context your brand is mentioned in AI-generated responses. Use this data to refine your GEO strategy.
